Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our printing services and policies.

We're open Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 8:30 PM, and weekends (Saturday-Sunday) from 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM. We're here to serve you with extended hours for your convenience!

Our ID RUSH services are designed for quick turnaround! Most ID cards can be completed within 30 minutes to 2 hours, depending on the complexity and current queue. For bulk orders, please allow 1-2 business days.

We accept most common file formats including PDF, JPEG, PNG, DOCX, and more. For best quality results, we recommend high-resolution files (300 DPI or higher). Our team can also help optimize your files for printing.

Yes! We offer competitive bulk pricing for large orders. Discounts typically apply for orders of 50+ pieces for ID cards, 100+ pages for documents, and 500+ pieces for business cards. Contact us for a custom quote!

Absolutely! Our LOGO HD service includes complete custom design from concept to final product. We provide 3 initial design concepts and 2 rounds of revisions to ensure you get exactly what you envision for your brand.

We accept cash payments at our shop location. For large orders or custom design work, we may require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the balance due upon completion.

Yes! We offer professional lamination services for IDs (25), A4 and short documents (35). Lamination helps protect your important documents from wear, moisture, and fading.

You can send files via email (jmbprintingservices12@gmail.com) or messaging apps for quotes and initial consultation. However, for final printing and pickup, please visit our shop in Balitoc, Calatagan, Batangas.

Our ID RUSH services vary by design complexity and features. ID RUSH 1-2 are basic designs (40-50), while ID RUSH 3-8 include more advanced layouts, colors, and security features (55-65). All include professional photo editing and layout design.

Currently, we operate from our shop location in Balitoc, Calatagan, Batangas. For large orders or special arrangements, please contact us at 09478188686 (SMART) or 09050869449 (TM) to discuss possible delivery options within the local area.
